# Phase 5 Evaluation Framework: Advanced Research & T5 Preparation

## 🎯 **Overall Assessment Strategy**
**Method**: Research excellence evaluation with international competition performance and T5 preparation tracking
**Frequency**: Weekly check-ins + 4-week milestone assessments + comprehensive phase reviews
**Grading Scale**: Mastery-based (0-4 scale: Not Started → Developing → Proficient → Advanced → Mastery)

---

## 📊 **Week-by-Week Evaluation Methods**

### **Weeks 125-128: Advanced Research Methods**
**Primary Evaluation**: Research Excellence & Methodology Mastery
- **Stanford/CMU Level Research** (40%):
  - Develop Stanford/CMU level research hypothesis
  - Design and execute advanced experiments
  - Analyze results with sophisticated statistical methods
  - **Success Criteria**: Research hypothesis advanced, experiments executed, analysis sophisticated

- **International Publication Standards** (35%):
  - Meet international journal publication standards
  - Prepare world-class figures and tables
  - Develop comprehensive literature review
  - **Success Criteria**: Publication standards met, figures world-class, literature comprehensive

- **Patent Application Skills** (25%):
  - Conduct comprehensive prior art search
  - Write international-quality patent application
  - Develop strong patent claims
  - **Success Criteria**: Prior art thorough, application international-quality, claims strong

**Evaluation Format**:
- ✅ Research assessment (Stanford/CMU level quality)
- ✅ Publication readiness (international journal standards)
- ✅ Patent preparation (international application quality)
