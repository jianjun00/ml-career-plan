## Protocol for Each Problem

**Per problem time budget: 2.5 hours**

1. **Solve independently** (90 min strict timer): no solution peeking. Submit to Kaggle judge or score locally.
2. **Record your metric** immediately after time is up.
3. **Read the gold/reference solution** (30 min): read every line; understand every design decision.
4. **Implement one key technique you missed** from the gold solution (30 min): write it yourself.
5. **Log the delta** (5 min): what was the single insight that separated your score from gold?
