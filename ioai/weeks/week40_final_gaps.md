## High-Value Focus Areas

These are what distinguishes gold from silver at IOAI, based on gold medalist analysis:

### Speed
- **Test**: can you get a working submission on any task type in < 30 minutes?
- **If no**: time yourself on 3 tasks this week; identify where time is lost
- Target: baseline in 25 min, not 45 min

### Mathematical Insight
- **Test**: given a mystery dataset, can you diagnose its structure in < 5 minutes with PCA/t-SNE?
- **If no**: revisit Week 17 and Week 35; practice daily with random datasets

### PyTorch Fluency
- **Test**: write a full Transformer encoder from scratch in < 20 minutes without documentation
- **If no**: practice writing Week 13's implementation from memory daily until automatic

### Baseline → Improvement Loop
- **Test**: can you submit a baseline in 30 min, then iterate 3 times in the next 60 min?
- **If no**: timed drills as in Week 26
