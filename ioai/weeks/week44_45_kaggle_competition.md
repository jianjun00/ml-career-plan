# Weeks 44–45 — Kaggle Grand Competition (Full Sprint)
## Phase 5: Peak Performance Maintenance (Weeks 44–45 of 48)

---

## Objective

Join a Kaggle competition in your **strongest domain** and pursue a competitive finish. This builds genuine competition experience under real leaderboard pressure.

**Target**: top 10% finish (significantly harder than the Week 34 target of top 20%)

---

## Week-by-Week Strategy

### Week 44: Foundation
- [ ] Day 1: Data exploration, baseline submission (< 30 min baseline)
- [ ] Day 2: Read top 10 public notebooks; understand what the best teams are doing
- [ ] Day 3–4: Implement your top 2 ideas from public notebooks (don't copy — understand and adapt)
- [ ] Day 5: Systematic ablation: one change at a time, log every score

### Week 45: Refinement & Ensemble
- [ ] Day 1–2: Hyperparameter tuning (Optuna) on your best model
- [ ] Day 3: Stacking or blending with 2–3 different model families
- [ ] Day 4: Feature engineering: what features do top notebooks create that you haven't?
- [ ] Day 5: Final ensemble, verify submission file format
- [ ] Weekend: submit final, document everything
