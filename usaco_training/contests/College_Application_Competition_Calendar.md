## 🏆 **Key Competition Timeline & Milestones**

### **🎯 Critical Competition Windows**

#### **USACO (USA Computing Olympiad)**
- **December Contest**: Early qualification opportunity
- **January Contest**: Mid-season advancement
- **February Contest**: Late-season improvement
- **March Contest**: Final qualification chance
- **US Open**: National championship (April/May)

#### **Major International Contests**
- **Google Code Jam**: Qualification (March), Rounds 1-3 (April-May)
- **Facebook Hacker Cup**: Qualification (January), Rounds 1-3 (February-March)
- **TopCoder Open**: Algorithm matches (year-round)
- **AtCoder**: Regular contests (weekly)

#### **College Application Deadlines**
- **Early Decision**: November 1 (Junior year achievements matter)
- **Regular Decision**: January 1 (Senior year fall achievements)
- **Scholarship Deadlines**: Various (competition achievements help)
