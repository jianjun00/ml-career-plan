## 🎯 **Division-Specific Training**

### 🥉 **Bronze Division Training**
**Duration**: 3 months | **Contests**: 4 | **Problems**: 300+

#### **Core Algorithms**
- **Sorting**: Bubble, Selection, Insertion, Merge, Quick, Heap
- **Searching**: Linear, Binary, Ternary Search
- **Data Structures**: Arrays, Vectors, Stacks, Queues
- **Basic Math**: Number theory, combinatorics, geometry

#### **Problem Types**
- **Simulation**: Step-by-step process simulation
- **Greedy**: Local optimal choices
- **Brute Force**: Exhaustive search
- **Implementation**: Careful coding challenges

#### **Training Schedule**
- **Daily**: 2-3 problems (1-2 hours)
- **Weekly**: 10-15 problems (8-10 hours)
- **Monthly**: 50-60 problems + contest (40-50 hours)

#### **Success Metrics**
- **Problem Speed**: < 30 minutes for bronze problems
- **Accuracy**: 90%+ on practice problems
- **Contest Score**: 800+ points consistently

### 🥈 **Silver Division Training**
**Duration**: 6 months | **Contests**: 6 | **Problems**: 500+

#### **Advanced Algorithms**
- **Dynamic Programming**: 1D, 2D, optimization
- **Graph Algorithms**: BFS, DFS, Dijkstra, Floyd-Warshall
- **Data Structures**: Sets, Maps, Priority Queues, Trees
- **Mathematical**: Number theory, combinatorics, probability

#### **Problem Types**
- **DP**: State design, transition optimization
- **Graph**: Shortest paths, connectivity, trees
- **Greedy**: Proof of optimality
- **Math**: Modular arithmetic, GCD, LCM, primes

#### **Training Schedule**
- **Daily**: 3-4 problems (2-3 hours)
- **Weekly**: 15-20 problems (12-15 hours)
- **Monthly**: 70-80 problems + contest (50-60 hours)

#### **Success Metrics**
