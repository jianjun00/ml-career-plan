# Detailed Monthly Training Plan
## Comprehensive Learning Materials & Practice Schedule for Top CS School Preparation

---

## 🎯 **Training Philosophy**

### **Core Principles**
- **Progressive Skill Development**: Systematic advancement through difficulty levels
- **Multiple Competition Preparation**: Balanced approach to different contest types
- **College Application Focus**: Achievement timing for maximum impact
- **World-Class Resources**: MIT, Stanford, CMU level materials
- **Consistent Practice**: Daily learning with weekly reinforcement

### **Monthly Structure**
- **Week 1**: Algorithm Learning & Foundation Building
- **Week 2**: Problem Practice & Implementation
- **Week 3**: Contest Simulation & Advanced Topics
- **Week 4**: Review, Projects & Competition Participation
