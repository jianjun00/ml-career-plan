# Phase 4 Evaluation Framework: College & Career Preparation

## 🎯 **Overall Assessment Strategy**
**Method**: College admission success + research internship placement + career readiness assessment
**Frequency**: Weekly application reviews + bi-weekly college prep milestones + monthly career development assessments
**Grading Scale**: Admission outcomes (acceptances/rejections) + internship placement + skill mastery assessments

---

## 📊 **Semester Evaluation Methods**

### **Fall 2027: College Applications (September - December)**

#### **Weeks 77-80: College Research & Selection**
**Primary Evaluation**: Research Quality and Decision Making
- **College Research Assessment** (40%):
  - Research 15+ universities with detailed analysis
  - Create comprehensive comparison spreadsheet
  - Document academic requirements and fit
  - **Success Criteria**: 15+ schools researched, spreadsheet complete, requirements documented

- **Decision Making Quality** (35%):
  - Create balanced college list (3-4 reach, 4-5 match, 3-4 safety)
  - Justify school selections with data
  - Create application strategy and timeline
  - **Success Criteria**: List balanced, justifications data-driven, strategy comprehensive

- **Network Development** (25%):
  - Connect with 5+ current students at target schools
  - Complete 3+ informational interviews
  - Document insights and recommendations
  - **Success Criteria**: Connections made, interviews completed, insights documented

**Evaluation Format**:
- ✅ Research documentation (spreadsheet and analysis)
- ✅ Decision review (college list and strategy)
- ✅ Network assessment (connections and interviews)

---
