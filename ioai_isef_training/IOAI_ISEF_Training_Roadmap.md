# IOAI & ISEF Training Roadmap
## Comprehensive Monthly Training Plan for T5 College Admissions

---

## 🎯 **Training Overview**

### **Objective**: Prepare high school students for IOAI and ISEF competitions
### **Duration**: 24 months (2 years comprehensive preparation)
### **Target**: Top 5 CS schools (MIT, Stanford, CMU, Berkeley, Cornell)
### **Focus**: AI/ML research, robotics, and scientific methodology

---

## 📅 **Monthly Training Structure**

### **Year 1: Foundation Building (Months 1-12)**

#### **Month 1-3: Fundamentals**
- **AI/ML Basics**: Python, data structures, algorithms
- **Research Methods**: Scientific methodology, literature review
- **Competition Understanding**: IOAI format, ISEF requirements

#### **Month 4-6: Technical Skills**
- **Machine Learning**: Supervised/unsupervised learning
- **Computer Vision**: Image processing, neural networks
- **Natural Language Processing**: Text analysis, transformers

#### **Month 7-9: Project Development**
- **Idea Generation**: Problem identification, innovation
- **Prototyping**: MVP development, testing
- **Documentation**: Research paper writing, presentation skills

#### **Month 10-12: Competition Preparation**
- **IOAI Training**: Scientific round, practical round practice
- **ISEF Preparation**: Project refinement, poster design
- **Mock Competitions**: Practice presentations, Q&A sessions

### **Year 2: Advanced Competition (Months 13-24)**

#### **Month 13-15: Specialization**
- **Advanced AI**: Deep learning, reinforcement learning
- **Robotics Integration**: Hardware-software systems
- **Domain Expertise**: Healthcare, environment, social impact

#### **Month 16-18: Research Intensive**
- **Original Research**: Novel problem solving
- **Collaboration**: Team projects, mentorship
- **Publication**: Conference submissions, journal papers

#### **Month 19-21: Competition Execution**
- **National Qualifiers**: IOAI national rounds, ISEF regional fairs
- **International Preparation**: Travel logistics, cultural readiness
- **Final Polish**: Project refinement, presentation practice

#### **Month 22-24: Post-Competition**
- **College Applications**: Competition experience integration
- **Networking**: Industry connections, university contacts
- **Legacy**: Mentorship, community contribution

---
