```
├── benchmarks/            # Mathematical evaluation benchmarks

```

### Mathematical Evaluation Metrics

#### Accuracy Metrics
- **Numerical Accuracy**: Exact match tolerance
- **Reasoning Accuracy**: Step-by-step correctness
- **Mathematical Rigor**: Proof validity assessment
- **Solution Completeness**: Full solution generation

#### Mathematical Benchmarks
- **MATH Dataset**: High school competition problems
- **GSM8K**: Grade school math word problems
- **Mathematical Reasoning**: Custom evaluation sets
- **Proof Benchmarks**: Formal theorem proving tasks
